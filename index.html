What does your service cost us as a tenant or occupier?

Absolutely nothing. Our fees are 100% landlord-paid — standard practice in commercial property brokerage where landlords build leasing commissions into their cost structure. You receive a fully-resourced, senior institutional brokerage team working exclusively in your interest at zero cost to your organisation.

What does "off-market" actually mean in the SA industrial context?

Off-market properties are spaces that institutional landlords and developers — Fortress REIT, Growthpoint, Equites, Attacq — make available privately through trusted broker relationships before or instead of public listing. This includes occupied properties with known upcoming vacancies, developer stock being pre-leased before slab pour, and institutional investor assets being repositioned. In our network, this represents over 70% of prime industrial stock transacted annually.

How quickly can you identify and present qualified properties?

For standard specifications (500m²–10,000m²) in primary nodes, we typically present a curated shortlist within 48 hours of brief completion. For technically complex requirements — high amperage manufacturing, ASRS-ready warehousing, or multi-site national rollouts — allow 5–10 business days for a properly vetted report. Our average across all engagement types is 14 days from brief to heads of terms.

Can you guarantee power-secure properties given load shedding?

Power security is one of the most common mandatory criteria we receive and is a core competency of our team. We specifically filter our network for power-secured facilities — confirmed solar PV capacity, battery storage ratings, diesel generator specifications, and Eskom supply security by node. We verify this data directly with landlords, not from listings. We can provide node-by-node power security assessments as part of the site audit.

What size range and deal types do you handle?

Our private network is deepest for requirements between 1,500m² and 50,000m². For requirements above 50,000m² we typically facilitate build-to-suit negotiations directly with institutional developers. We handle leasing, renewals, subleases, land acquisition, and turnkey build-to-suit contracts. We do not handle retail or office property — we are exclusively industrial.

Which regions and industrial nodes do you cover nationally?

We have embedded senior broker teams in Johannesburg, Pretoria, Cape Town, and Durban with direct network access across all primary industrial nodes — Pomona, Kempton Park, Germiston, Centurion, Midrand, Airport Industria, Epping, Montague Gardens, Pinetown, Riverhorse Valley, and Dube TradePort. For secondary markets including Gqeberha, East London, Polokwane, and Rustenburg, we activate our extended partner network.
